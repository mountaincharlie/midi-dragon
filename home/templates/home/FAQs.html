FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need to pay to have an account with midiDRAGON?

No. Registering for an account is completely free! midiDRAGON doesn't run any subscription services, so you won't be asked for any payment information until you place an order for any Pre-made and/or Custom Songs.

How soon will i get access to my purchased Pre-made Songs?

ASAP! You will be sent a confirmation email whenever you complete an order and if your order contains any Pre-made Songs, there will be links included to download the songs straight away.

Do I need to register for an account inorder to design a custom song?

Yes. In order to be able to manage and maintain your custom songs you'll need a place to store them. With a midiDRAGON account you will have your own Dashboard to view your Details, Draft Projects, Projects in-progress, Completed Projects and Order History.

How long will it take for my Custom Song to be created?

Completion time for Custom Songs depends on the complexity of the project, the number of review sessions included or purchased and how busy midiDRAGON is with other custom projects.
Once midiDRAGON has recieved and reviewed your Custom Song Design, they will be in contact to let you know an estimated completion time.

Can I get a refund?

Purchases of any Pre-made Songs are not eligible for refund. For information on the Custom Song refund policy, please see our Terms of Service

Does midiDRAGON own all the music on the site?

Yes. All songs and musical content on the midiDRAGON site has been created and is owned fully by midiDRAGON.

Are midiDRAGON's Pre-made and Custom Songs Royalty Free?

Yes.

What does midiDRAGON's songs being Royalty Free mean?

The 2 key things you need to know about Royalty Free music are:

  1. You only have to pay for any of midiDRAGON's Pre-made or Custom Songs once and then you can use those songs anywhere, as many times as you want, forever.
  2. You don't own the rights to any of the Pre-made or Custom Songs you buy - they still belong to midiDRAGON so you can't sell on any of the songs or pass them off as your own work.

For full details please see our Terms of Service.
